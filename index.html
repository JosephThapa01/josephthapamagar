About Me

I’m an Embedded Systems and Robotics Engineer specializing in C programming, microcontroller systems, motion control, and automation. I design intelligent hardware from IoT devices to advanced robotic mechanisms—using platforms like AVR, STM32, ESP32, and Raspberry Pi. With experience in robot design, PCB development, and control systems, I’ve built solutions ranging from 4G data loggers and quadruped robots to drink-serving robotic arms, cattle monitoring and electric vehicles.

I love building real-world systems that blend mechanical design, electronics, and intelligent control tackling complex challenges and turning ideas into working prototypes.
